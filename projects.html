Heber City, Utah

City-Wide Trip Hazard Inventory

Completed 2025

The Challenge

The city needed a systematic way to identify trip hazards to prioritize grinding and replacement projects. They required a format that could easily import into their existing GIS viewer.

The Solution

QJF deployed field teams to walk the designated sectors. We identified hazards, classified them by severity (Low/Medium/High), and noted specific causes such as tree roots.

Deliverables:
  • CSV Database with GPS Lat/Long
  • High-Resolution Photo Links
  • Severity & Cause Classification
